# Base Topics & Variant Axes

This file defines the foundational weekly topic pools and the 5 variant axes to apply when generating per-channel content.

## Variant Axes (apply per channel/week)
1. **Language**: English, Spanish, Spanglish, plus creator-specific English variants.
2. **Tone/Vibe**: warm devotional, playful/cheeky, reflective poetic, uplifting motivational, relaxed social.
3. **Education Level**: simple/accessible, moderate, artful/advanced.
4. **Genre Mix**: per channel profile (see channel lists).
5. **Humor/Intensity**: light humor vs. serious/earnest, aligned to creator style.

## Johannes (Austria) - 7 Base Topics
1. **Faith & Daily Grace**: Christian values in everyday life, humility, mercy.
2. **Nature as Parable**: mountains, rivers, wind, seasons as spiritual mirrors.
3. **Craft & Integrity**: things well made, precision, artistry, patience.
4. **Life Stories & Moral Turns**: narrative storytelling with a moral or spiritual insight.
5. **Community & Kindness**: family, neighbor love, service, gratitude.
6. **Pilgrimage & Inner Journey**: reflection, silence, growth, spiritual travel.
7. **Joy & Celebration**: gratitude, playfulness, hope, and community joy.

## Daniel (Dominican Republic) - 7 Base Topics
1. **Barrio Life & Community Humor**: local observations, social niche jokes.
2. **Beach & Tropical Rhythm**: daily life near the coast, breeze, sun.
3. **Romance & Everyday Love**: tender or playful love stories.
